IEEE E-board Team Descriptions!

Academic Team

The academic team helps cultivate relationships among students and faculty in both ECE and the wider Engineering school. They maintain relationships with professors and provide advising on academic topics including course selection, research, and grad school. Furthermore, they help manage an ECE mentorship program.

Corporate Team

The Corporate team facilitates all events with engineering and tech companies, including company sponsored information sessions, tech talks, and more! They oversee all logistics related to each event, and help establish corporate partnerships with companies such as AMD, MITRE, and Lyft.

Publicity Team

Publicity team manages IEEE's public image and branding. This includes designing graphics and marketing material for events over Facebook, TV screens, apparel, and other forms of media that helps expand IEEE's influence on campus. Every year, the publicity team designs ECE stickers and Tshirts for ECE day.

Social Team

The social team helps foster community both within the wider ECE community and among E-Board members. They help organize outreach events like ice cream socials, holiday parties, and trivia nights. The social team also puts together fun events for the E-Board, like secret santa, board game nights, and team dinners.

Web Team

The web team designs, develops, and maintains the IEEE Cornell website. Team members gain experience with user interface design, web development, and deployment best practices. Currently, the website is being redesigned so future web team members will have the opportunity to create IEEE's new digital identity.
