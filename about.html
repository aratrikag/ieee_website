WHO WE ARE

Cornell IEEE is a student led branch of the national Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineerings(IEEE) within the Cornell's ECE department. As a studnet branch we subscribe to the IEE Core Values of trust, growth and nuturing, glocal community building, partnership, service to humanity and integrity in action. Our mission is to build a strong collaborative community of STEM students (undergraduate and graduate) and faculty at Cornell University by hosting social, networking, professional development, educational workshops and outreach events. We also regularly host corporate outreach events to provide companies access to Cornell ECE and CS students.
